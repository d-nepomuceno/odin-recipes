Poke Recipe

A traditional Hawaiian ahi poke bowl recipe made with sashimi grade ahi tuna, sweet onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, chopped macadamia nuts, and scallions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh sashimi-grade ahi steak
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 3/4 teaspoon Hawaiian salt
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced Maui or yellow onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/8 teaspoon gochugaru
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped toasted macadamia nuts
  • 2 cups steamd rice

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the cubed ahi, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, Maui onion, green onions, gochugaru, and toasted macadamia nuts and gently toss with your hands or a spoon. Adjust the seasoning to your liking.
  2. Serve over rice and enjoy immediately.
